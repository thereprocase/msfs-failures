# OnAir Company

**URL:** https://onair.company/

## Overview

OnAir describes itself as "The most complete economic platform for flight simulators. From freelance pilot to full airline management, in a persistent shared world."

## Career Model

### Dual Path System
Players can choose between:
1. **Freelance Pilot** - Accept missions to earn XP and money, continue indefinitely without commitment
2. **Airline Owner** - Use accumulated progress to launch and manage a full airline operation

Key quote: "Begin as a freelance pilot and take on missions to earn XP and money. Keep flying as freelance pilot or use your progress to launch your own airline, it's your call."

## Economy System

### Mission Generation
- Dynamically generated jobs based on player aircraft and operational range
- Mission types: cargo, passenger transport, sightseeing, urgent contracts
- AI crew can handle flights while player is offline

### Persistent Operations
- **Offline Progression**: "Your AI crews continue flights, complete jobs and generate income while you're away"
- **Industries**: Industries keep producing, transforming, and delivering automatically
- **Shared World**: Other pilots run airlines in real time within same persistent environment

### Financial Management
- Buy, maintain, and refuel aircraft
- Pay salaries and balance costs
- Track profits and losses
- Manage aircraft leases and maintenance

## Fleet & Airline Management

### Aircraft Operations
- Aircraft purchases and fleet acquisition
- Maintenance and refueling systems
- Crew hiring and assignment
- Work order automation

### Scheduled Routes
- Create professional airline schedules
- Recurring flights with AI crews
- Route optimization and management

## Infrastructure & Airports

### FBO/Airport Ownership
- Buy and own airports
- Apply boosters to reduce fuel costs
- Reduce loading times
- Improve overall airport operations

## Pricing & Access

- **7-Day Free Trial** (no credit card required)
- **24-Hour Freelance Passes** (new on-demand option)
- Subscription-based paid tiers (specific costs not available)
- Free-to-try model: "Try the full experience. Purchase only when you're ready."

## Platform Compatibility

Supports:
- Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024/2020
- X-Plane 12/11
- Prepar3D
- FSX

## Key Design Patterns

1. **Flexible Entry**: Low barrier to entry with free trial and optional subscription
2. **Choice Architecture**: Players choose engagement level (freelance vs. ownership)
3. **Offline Automation**: AI crews allow progression without active play
4. **Multiplayer Persistence**: Shared world with real player economies
5. **Infrastructure Ownership**: Airport/FBO mechanics add depth beyond aircraft
