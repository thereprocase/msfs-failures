# Air Hauler 2

**Vendor:** Just Flight  
**URL:** https://www.justflight.com/product/air-hauler-2  
**Price:** $49.49 USD

## Overview

Air Hauler 2 enables players to establish and operate their own freight and passenger aviation company. It balances cockpit flying with business management, offering complete control "from landings to leases, flight plans to finances, and repairs to recruitment."

## Business Management Features

### Financial Systems
- Aircraft purchases (new and used)
- Aircraft leasing with flexible terms
- Fuel costs and management
- Landing fees and airport charges
- Loan management and financing
- Profit/loss tracking and accounting

### Infrastructure Development
- Build and upgrade bases
- Fuel depot construction
- Storage facility expansion
- Factory establishment for commodity manufacturing
- Commodity trading between locations

### Operational Management
- Recruit and manage virtual airline crews
- Pilot hiring system
- AI pilot development with 18-skill progression tree
- Performance tracking across five skill categories
- Type rating system (pilots require qualifications for aircraft)

## Flight Operations

### Mission Types
- Cargo delivery jobs with varying complexity
- Passenger transport contracts
- Humanitarian relief missions (responding to real-world disasters)
- Special missions for airport contacts
- Commodity trading runs

### Delivery & Logistics
- Aircraft delivery options (delivery service or self-ferry)
- New and used aircraft marketplace
- Job selection based on aircraft type and range

## Fleet Management

### Aircraft Operations
- Purchase aircraft (new or used)
- Type rating system requiring pilot qualifications
- Self-ferry capability to new bases
- Aircraft-specific job eligibility

### Crew Development System
- 18-skill development tree for AI pilots
- Five skill categories for performance tracking
- Pilot recruitment and assignment
- Performance-based pilot progression

## Key Design Patterns

1. **Integrated Cockpit + Management**: Flying and business management deeply integrated
2. **Crew Development**: AI pilots have progression and specialization
3. **Realistic Costs**: Aircraft purchases, fuel, maintenance, landing fees all factor into profit
4. **Specialization Gating**: Type rating system prevents unlimited aircraft use
5. **Disaster Response**: Real-world event integration (humanitarian missions)
6. **Commodity Markets**: Factory and trading mechanics add economic depth
7. **Multiplayer Recruitment**: Other players can be recruited as virtual pilots
