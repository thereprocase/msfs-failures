## Key Economic Primitives by System

### OnAir
- Unit: Money (earned via missions)
- Costs: Aircraft maintenance, fuel, crew salaries
- Revenue: Dynamic mission payouts
- Persistent: Yes (continues offline)
- Scale: Airline operations (multiple aircraft, multiple crews)

### FSEconomy
- Unit: Virtual currency (mission payouts)
- Costs: Aircraft rental, fuel, operational expenses
- Revenue: Cargo/passenger mission payouts
- Persistent: Yes (player-driven state)
- Scale: Individual to fleet (rental or owned aircraft)

### Air Hauler 2
- Unit: Money (mission payouts, commodity trading)
- Costs: Aircraft purchase/lease, fuel, landing fees, crew salaries, maintenance
- Revenue: Cargo, passenger, special missions
- Persistent: Yes (career across multiple sessions)
- Scale: Airline growth (from startup to multi-aircraft operation)

### Neofly
- Data unavailable (website unreachable)
